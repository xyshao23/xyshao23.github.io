About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. student (2023 intake) in Electronic Information at Tsinghua University Shenzhen International Graduate School (SIGS). Simultaneously, I work as an AI Algorithm Researcher at the Central Software Institute, Huawei 2012 Lab.

My research lies at the intersection of Embodied AI and Robotics. I am particularly driven by the challenge of moving beyond traditional end-to-end VLA models by exploring decoupled perception-control architectures, generative models for physical decision-making, and pushing the boundaries of imitation learning in low-data regimes. I have extensive experience in building robust pipelines for long-horizon complex robotic manipulations using high-framerate HDF5 video streams.
